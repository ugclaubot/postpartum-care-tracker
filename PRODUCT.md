# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Family caregivers and postpartum mothers who need a calm way to track maternal recovery, test schedules, symptoms, lab results, and doctor follow-up after delivery. The tool is designed for collaboration with an obstetrician or qualified clinician, not for self-diagnosis.

## Product Purpose

Postpartum Care Tracker turns delivery date, risk factors, symptoms, and submitted test results into an organized dashboard: what to test, when to test it, what results may mean, and what to discuss next with a clinician. Success means fewer missed follow-ups, clearer doctor conversations, and safer escalation when warning signs appear.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, protective.

## Anti-references

This should not look like a wellness influencer app, a noisy hospital EHR, or a generic AI chatbot. It should avoid decorative gradients, vague supplement promises, diagnosis-like language, and any UI that makes clinical warnings feel casual.

## Design Principles

1. Safety before convenience: urgent symptoms and severe values must surface immediately.
2. Explain without diagnosing: every suggestion must say what to discuss or verify, not prescribe treatment.
3. Make trends visible: one result matters, but repeated results and context matter more.
4. Reduce family confusion: translate test names into plain meaning and next actions.
5. Keep private data local by default until a backend is intentionally added.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-friendly controls, reduced-motion support, clear focus states, plain medical language, and conservative color use that does not rely on color alone for status.
